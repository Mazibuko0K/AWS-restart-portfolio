# AWS SimuLearn: [Databases in Practice]

In this AWS SimuLearn project, I tackled an insurance company's need to free their DBAs from tedious patching and management by designing a database solution that boosts availability and efficiency. I put it together by consulting with an AI customer to lock in the requirements, then built and validated the whole thing in a live AWS console. The takeaway? Real skills in automating database operations and translating business pain points into scalable cloud architecture.
