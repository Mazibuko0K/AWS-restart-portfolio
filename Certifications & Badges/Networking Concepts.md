# AWS SimuLearn: [Networking Concepts

For this AWS SimuLearn project, I designed a secure network architecture for a bank to tightly control traffic between their internal systems and the internet. After walking through requirements with an AI customer, I built and validated the solution in a live AWS console—locking down connectivity the way it'd be done in the real world. Bottom line: sharpened my ability to architect secure, production-ready networks while keeping the client's business needs front and center.
